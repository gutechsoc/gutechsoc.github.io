Do You Have The GUTS?

5th February 2021

Online

After postponing this event due to the pandemic we are pleased to finally welcome you to our flagship event - Do You Have The GUTS? 2021.
This annual University of Glasgow hackathon is the biggest event hosted by the Glasgow University Tech Society and is held in collaboration with the School of Computing Science along with some of Glasgow's (and the world's!) biggest and most influential tech companies.
So come along for 48 hours of hackathon! Make friends, solve challenges and virtually rub shoulders with both industry and academia.
After the success of Code Olympics in October 2020, we will also run this hack using Discord as the main platform of communication and we will be posting information there in the run up to the event.
